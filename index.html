About Me

I am a second year graduate student at the University of Minnesota, interested in algebraic combinatorics and commutative algebra. I am currently reading with Pasha Pylyavskyy on the topic of Soergel bimodules and Kazhdan-Lusztig theory. I graduated from San Diego State University in 2021, where I worked with Christopher O'Neill on problems relating to numerical semigroups and polytopes.

Recent Activities

  • (Fall 2023) I am organizing Student Combinatorics and Algebra Seminar with fellow graduate students Miranda Moore and Sylvester Zhang
  • (Summer 2023) I participated in the Macaulay 2 Workshop at UMN, where I contributed to the Alternating Sign Matrix Varieties package mentored by Ayah Almousa and Patricia Klein
  • (Summer 2023) I participated in MRWAC at UMN, a research workshop in algebra and combinatorics.
  • (Spring 2023) I attended the Syzygies and Regularity workshop at UIC
