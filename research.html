Interests

My research interests are mainly centered around algebraic combinatorics and commutative algebra, especially problems that have applications to representation theory and algebraic geometry. Some combinatorial objects I like thinking about are

  • Symmetric functions
  • Kazhdan-Lusztig polynomials
  • Numerical semigroups
